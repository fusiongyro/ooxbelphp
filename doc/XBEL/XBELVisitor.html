		visitAlias (line 47)
	
 
	
	

Called when an alias is visited.


Note: the target of an alias is not automatically visited.  However, because the target must have an ID, it will be visited in its  natural setting at one point or another during processing, so this method  is probably only interesting to you if you're concerned primarily with  the structure of the bookmarks rather than the contents of them.
